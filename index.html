About SFHS

San Francisco High School was established on June 9, 1954 and serves learners in Quezon City as a public secondary institution for Grades 7 through 12.

The school was named Don Mariano Marcos High School from 1975 to 1986 before returning to its present name. Today, SFHS continues to uphold its legacy of accessible, quality public education.
